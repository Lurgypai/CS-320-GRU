How To Play



This game follows most standards of American checkers with some adaptations where,

•Pawns are only able to move diagonally and forward one square.

•After selecting a piece possible positions it can move to will be highlighted on the board.

•To capture a piece you must jump over it (diagonally), multi-jumps are allowed but they must all capture one piece per jump.

•It is not enforced that a player jumps a piece and instead can opt not to, unless they are committing a multi-jump.

•Captured pieces are then removed from the board and game.

•Once a piece reaches the other side of the board it becomes a King.

•King pieces follow the same rules however they are allowed to move to squares that are diagonally backwards and jump backwards.

•To win you must capture all enemy pieces or prohibit the other player from being able to move any of their pieces.

To start playing,

•Choose the "Play Game" option when you wish to play, feel free to enter any username you would like.

•From this point you need to first enter your room join code, if you plan to host it will be how others connect to you.

•If you are hosting wait until the other player joins, those joining will need to enter the join code of the person hosting.

•Once they connect this should setup the game page where the board will be displayed, Black will always move first.

•After completing a game players can feel free to return to the landing page via the home button.

When you are ready to play, return with the button below!
