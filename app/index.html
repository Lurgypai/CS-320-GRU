How To Play



This game follows the standards of American checkers where,

•Pawns are only able to move diagonally and forward one square.
•To capture a piece you must jump over it (diagonally), multi-jumps are allowed but they must all capture one piece per jump.
•Captured pieces are then removed from the board and game.
•Once a piece reaches the other side of the board it becomes a King.
•King pieces follow the same rules however they are allowed to move to squares that are diagonally backwards and jump backwards.
•To win you must capture all enemy pieces or prohibit the other player from being able to move any of their pieces.

To start playing,

•Choose the game option you wish to play, against the AI specify difficulty, against a person choose host or join.
•If you are hosting wait until the other player joins, if you are joining then you will need to enter the username of the person hosting.
•Once they connect this should transport you to the game page where the board will be displayed, Black will always move first.
•After completing a game the menu at the top will allow players to reset the game to play again or cancel the game.
